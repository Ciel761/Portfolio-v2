Siyi Zhu

Siyi Zhu is a PhD Candidate at the University of Maryland, studying Human-computer Interaction. His research explores how emergying technologies can support sensemaking work in scholarly research. He combines design, qualitative methods to investigate user experience how we can better support researchers in navigating complex information spaces.
